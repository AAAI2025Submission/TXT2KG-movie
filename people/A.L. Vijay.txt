Mini Bio
A.L. Vijay was born on June 18, 1979 in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, India. He is a director and writer, known for Deiva Thirumagal (2011), Thalaivi (2021) and Madrasapattinam (2010). He was previously married to Amala Paul.
